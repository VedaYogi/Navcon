NEXT GENERATION NAVIGATION AND GUIDANCE CONTROL SYSTEM FOR OUR VISUALLY IMPAIRED FRIENDS

Combining state of the art object detection neural nets and deep Q learning models with Virtual Localisation and Mapping and Robotic Operating system,
we strive to bring visual independence for our visually impaired friends

WHO ARE WE

We are group of young Artificial Iintelligence Specialist who want to leverage the power of AI and technology to bring good to society.

Our Navcon suite of products helps our visually impaired friends in both indoor and outdoor navigation, text recognition as well as environment sensing.

Indoor and Outdoor NavigationUsing a combination of object detection neural nets, reinforcement learning, virtual simultaneous localisation and mapping and robotic operating system, Navcon strives to provide navigation guidance in every location.

Simple HardwareNavcon employs a small but powerful Jetson Nano development kit which houses a 128 core Maxwell GPU for processing the multiple neural networks in parallel. Additionally, Navcon is packaged with a customised user interface as well as Jabra headset.

Automation with Community SupportNavcon suite of products support an automated data ingestion pipeline that relises on community support and volunteer organisations to map objects and indoor locations in our pipeline.
